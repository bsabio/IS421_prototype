LearnNow Secures $5M Seed Backed by Education Fund

OrdoAI News Desk • 2026-02-02

CyberShield, a New York-based cybersecurity startup, has successfully raised $12 million in a Series A funding round led by Security Ventures. The announcement, made on February 1, 2026, marks a significant milestone for the company as it seeks to advance its next-generation security platform designed for enterprise clients. Alongside Security Ventures, the round also saw participation from Tech Capital and several industry investors, underscoring strong confidence in CyberShield’s technology and growth potential.

Founded with a focus on leveraging artificial intelligence to enhance cybersecurity, CyberShield is developing an AI-powered threat detection and response platform. This platform aims to help enterprises identify and mitigate cyber threats more quickly and effectively than traditional security solutions. As cyberattacks continue to grow in sophistication and frequency, the demand for advanced, automated defense systems is increasing, positioning CyberShield well within a competitive market.

The $12 million Series A injection will enable CyberShield to accelerate product development and expand its team, particularly in engineering and research. With backing from Security Ventures, a leading investor known for supporting innovative cybersecurity companies, CyberShield gains not only capital but also strategic guidance and industry connections crucial for scaling operations.

Tech Capital and other industry investors’ involvement further highlights the startup’s appeal to a broad range of stakeholders interested in next-generation cybersecurity technologies. This diverse investor base may also facilitate partnerships and customer acquisition as CyberShield moves toward commercial deployment of its platform.

CyberShield’s funding round reflects a broader trend in the cybersecurity sector, where startups harnessing AI and machine learning are attracting significant investment. Enterprises are increasingly prioritizing proactive and intelligent security solutions to protect sensitive data and infrastructure from evolving threats.

As CyberShield prepares to bring its AI-driven platform to market, the company is poised to contribute to the ongoing transformation of cybersecurity strategies. The successful Series A round not only provides necessary resources but also validates CyberShield’s approach in a rapidly changing digital landscape.
