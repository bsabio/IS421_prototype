NYC Founders & Funders Meetup

AI Factory News Desk • Feb 15, 2026 6:00 PM

The NYC Founders & Funders Meetup is set to take place on February 15, 2026, at 6:00 PM, offering a valuable opportunity for tech entrepreneurs and investors to connect in person. The event will be held at The Yard in the Lower East Side neighborhood of New York City, a popular spot known for fostering creative and professional collaboration. Best of all, attendance is completely free, removing any financial barriers for those looking to expand their networks.

This monthly gathering is designed specifically for founders, venture capitalists, and angel investors who want to build meaningful relationships within the city’s vibrant tech ecosystem. While the startup world is often portrayed as a race for funding and innovation, events like this emphasize the importance of simply showing up and engaging with the community. The people who attend regularly aren’t necessarily more talented or better connected—they just make the effort to be present.

Networking events such as the NYC Founders & Funders Meetup provide a unique chance to meet potential partners, mentors, and backers in an informal setting. For early-stage founders, these connections can be crucial in securing investment or gaining advice on scaling their businesses. For investors, it’s an opportunity to discover emerging startups and evaluate promising ideas firsthand.

The timing of the meetup is also significant. With the tech industry constantly evolving, waiting too long to get involved can mean missing out on key opportunities. Each month that passes without making new connections is a month competitors are moving ahead. This event encourages those in the startup scene to stop waiting for the “perfect time” and instead take action now.

The venue, The Yard - Lower East Side, is known for its collaborative atmosphere and modern amenities, making it an ideal place for entrepreneurs and investors to mingle and exchange ideas. Its location in one of NYC’s most dynamic neighborhoods adds to the appeal, attracting a diverse crowd from across the city’s tech community.

In summary, the NYC Founders & Funders Meetup on February 15 offers a free, accessible platform for founders and investors to come together, share insights, and build the relationships that can drive success. For anyone involved in the New York City tech scene, attending this event could be a pivotal step forward.
