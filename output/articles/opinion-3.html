Entrepreneur First NYC

OrdoAI News Desk • March 03, 2026

Entrepreneur First has launched its New York City program, offering a unique opportunity for exceptional individuals focused on deep tech and artificial intelligence to accelerate their startup journeys. This pre-team accelerator is designed specifically for founders who are tackling some of the most complex and impactful challenges in these cutting-edge fields.

Unlike traditional accelerators that work with established teams or companies, Entrepreneur First NYC seeks out talented individuals before they have formed a team or developed a product. The program helps these founders find co-founders, develop their ideas, and build companies from the ground up. This approach has proven successful in other Entrepreneur First locations around the world, making the New York program a promising option for deep tech entrepreneurs.

Located in the heart of New York City, Entrepreneur First NYC is positioned to tap into the city’s growing ecosystem of technology innovation. The program’s focus on deep tech and AI reflects the increasing importance of these sectors in addressing global challenges, from healthcare and energy to cybersecurity and beyond. By supporting founders at the earliest stage, Entrepreneur First aims to nurture breakthroughs that might otherwise struggle to find support.

Applications for the current cohort are now open, and interested individuals are encouraged to apply as soon as possible. These cohorts tend to fill quickly, given the program’s reputation and the high demand among founders working in deep tech. The application process is accessible online at joinef.com, where candidates can learn more about the program and submit their applications.

For entrepreneurs working on advanced technologies and seeking a supportive environment to build their startup from scratch, Entrepreneur First NYC offers a compelling path forward. With its emphasis on exceptional talent and deep tech innovation, the program could be the launchpad for the next generation of transformative companies emerging from New York City.
