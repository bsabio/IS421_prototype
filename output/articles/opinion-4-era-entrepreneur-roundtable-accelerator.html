ERA (Entrepreneur Roundtable Accelerator)

AI Factory News Desk • February 20, 2026

For entrepreneurs building businesses in the B2B, SaaS, and marketplace sectors, the Entrepreneur Roundtable Accelerator (ERA) in New York City presents a compelling opportunity. As the largest accelerator in NYC, ERA has established a strong reputation for working closely with founders who are addressing some of the most significant challenges in these industries. With applications currently open, startups that fit this profile should consider applying soon, as spots in their cohorts tend to fill quickly.

ERA offers a well-structured program designed to help early-stage companies scale efficiently. The accelerator provides a $100,000 investment in exchange for 6% equity, giving startups both capital and access to a broad network of mentors, investors, and industry experts. This combination of funding and support can be crucial for young companies navigating the competitive landscape of B2B software and marketplace platforms.

Located in the heart of New York City, ERA benefits from its proximity to a vibrant tech ecosystem and a diverse pool of talent. The accelerator’s focus on B2B, SaaS, and marketplaces aligns with some of the fastest-growing segments in the tech industry, where innovation is driving significant transformation. Founders accepted into the program gain not only financial backing but also strategic guidance tailored to their specific business models and market challenges.

The timing to apply is particularly important. Accelerators like ERA operate on a cohort basis, meaning that startups enter the program in groups and progress through the curriculum together. This cohort model fosters collaboration and peer learning, but it also means that opportunities are limited and competitive. Entrepreneurs interested in participating should visit ERA’s website to submit their applications promptly.

In summary, ERA represents a valuable gateway for startups aiming to make a mark in B2B, SaaS, and marketplace sectors. By offering a combination of investment, mentorship, and community, ERA helps founders accelerate their growth and increase their chances of long-term success. For those building in these spaces, applying to ERA could be a pivotal step in their entrepreneurial journey.
